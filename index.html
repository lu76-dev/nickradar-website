// the process
three steps.
one connection.
01
spot the sticker
someone around you is wearing a sticker: NR: john_innsbruck

that's their nickradar nickname. no app required to see it. no QR code scanner needed. just your eyes.
02
search the nickname
open nickradar → type the nickname → see their public profile.

you see their bio, their location distance. you decide if you want to send a chat request.

the request includes a short message — make it count. it's almost already paid for.
03
they decide
the target receives your request and message. they have 12 hours to respond.

YES → both get charged, 48h chat opens immediately.
NO → permanently blocked, no charge.
no answer → request expires, no charge, you can try again later.
04
48 hours to connect
chat is open for 48 hours. use it to exchange contacts, agree to meet, move to whatsapp.

after 48h the chat disappears forever. nickradar's job is done.

// our story
built for the
real world.
nickradar was born from a simple frustration: you meet someone interesting — on the train, at a café, at an event — and there's no respectful way to say "I'd like to stay in touch."
dating apps put you in a digital bubble, swiping through profiles of people you've never met. nickradar flips this completely: you meet first, then you connect digitally. the physical world comes first.

"the app that wants to make itself unnecessary."

our mission is simple: bridge the gap between a real-world encounter and a digital connection — then get out of the way. once you're on whatsapp, we've done our job.
nickradar is bootstrapped, based in austria, and built with the EU's values in mind: privacy first, transparency always, no dark patterns.
physical → digital
real encounters come first. the app is the bridge, not the destination.
no algorithm
we don't decide who you meet. reality does.
privacy by design
no public profiles, no location tracking, no data selling.
fair economics
money flows only when both sides benefit. no exploitation.
eu made
built in austria, gdpr compliant, no american data practices.
bootstrapped
no investors, no pressure to grow at any cost. we grow when it makes sense.
