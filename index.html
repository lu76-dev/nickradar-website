// the process
one moment.
one shot.
real.
01
spot the nickname
someone shows their NR nickname — sticker, patch, t-shirt, phone case, bike, bag. anything.

example:
NR: eva_queen_2008
NR: johnny_cash_2006

02
search and send a request
open nickradar, type the nickname, you are the Seeker now, check the profile, send a CONTACTING REQUEST. your request includes: a personal message (10–300 chars) + your location at time of sending.

sending is free. you only pay if the Target says YES.
03
Target responds — or not
the request is active for 12 hours. three outcomes:

YES => both pay, 48h chat opens immediately.
NO => request denied. permanently blocked, both directions. no charge.
NO REPLY => expires after 12h. no charge. both can try again.
04
48 hours to connect
talk, agree to meet, exchange numbers — whatever makes sense.

after 48h the chat disappears forever. nickradar's job is done. what comes next is up to you.
